<div class="jp-section">
  <div class="container">
    <div class="jp-section-header">
      <h2>Current Research Directions</h2>
      <p class="jp-subtitle">Our active research spans multimodal perception, efficient and hardware-aware AI, and learning-based low-level vision. A number of these efforts are conducted with industry partners or are currently under peer review, so detailed results and publications will be released as the work matures.</p>
    </div>

    <div class="research-directions-grid">
      <div class="direction-card" data-aos="fade-up">
        <div class="direction-icon"><i class="fas fa-language"></i></div>
        <h3>Vision–Language Models</h3>
        <p>Multimodal vision–language models (VLMs) for joint image–text understanding, visual grounding, and reasoning, including the adaptation and alignment of large VLMs for domain-specific perception.</p>
      </div>

      <div class="direction-card" data-aos="fade-up">
        <div class="direction-icon"><i class="fas fa-object-group"></i></div>
        <h3>Object Detection &amp; Segmentation</h3>
        <p>Robust object detection and semantic/instance segmentation, with an emphasis on accuracy and reliability under challenging, real-world visual conditions.</p>
      </div>

      <div class="direction-card" data-aos="fade-up">
        <div class="direction-icon"><i class="fas fa-bolt"></i></div>
        <h3>Efficient AI on Edge Devices</h3>
        <p>Lightweight neural architectures together with model compression and quantization for low-latency, energy-efficient inference on resource-constrained edge platforms.</p>
      </div>

      <div class="direction-card" data-aos="fade-up">
        <div class="direction-icon"><i class="fas fa-microchip"></i></div>
        <h3>Hardware–Software Co-Design</h3>
        <p>Designing hardware-friendly AI models from a deployment-aware perspective, jointly optimizing algorithms and accelerators for SoC/FPGA targets under memory, throughput, and power budgets.</p>
      </div>

      <div class="direction-card" data-aos="fade-up">
        <div class="direction-icon"><i class="fas fa-camera-retro"></i></div>
        <h3>RAW-Domain Learning &amp; AI-ISP</h3>
        <p>AI-based image signal processing that learns directly in the RAW sensor domain, rather than the conventional sRGB domain, to fully exploit sensor information for downstream vision tasks.</p>
      </div>

      <div class="direction-card" data-aos="fade-up">
        <div class="direction-icon"><i class="fas fa-layer-group"></i></div>
        <h3>Foundation Vision Models for Low-Level Vision</h3>
        <p>Adapting large-scale foundation vision models as priors and backbones for downstream low-level vision applications such as restoration, enhancement, and reconstruction.</p>
      </div>

      <div class="direction-card" data-aos="fade-up">
        <div class="direction-icon"><i class="fas fa-palette"></i></div>
        <h3>Implicit Neural Color Representations</h3>
        <p>Implicit neural representation (INR)-based 3D look-up tables (3D LUTs) for learnable color transformation, color-space conversion, and color-difference / chromatic-aberration correction.</p>
      </div>

      <div class="direction-card" data-aos="fade-up">
        <div class="direction-icon"><i class="fas fa-car-side"></i></div>
        <h3>Cross-Camera Auto-Annotation for Autonomous Driving</h3>
        <p>Automatic annotation of multi-camera, surround-view autonomous-driving imagery, exploiting cross-camera geometric and temporal consistency to generate high-quality perception labels while greatly reducing manual labeling effort.</p>
      </div>
    </div>
  </div>
</div>
