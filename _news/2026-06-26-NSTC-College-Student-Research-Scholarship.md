Congratulations to undergraduate research student Hsien-Chin Su (蘇賢晉), under the guidance of Prof. Li-Wei Kang (康立威), for receiving the NSTC College Student Research Scholarship (大專生補助計劃).

<br>Project Title: "Hybrid Document Image Dewarping Combining Fast Fourier Convolution and Variational Physical Constraints" (融合快速傅立葉卷積與變分物理約束之混合式文件影像去彎曲研究)

<br>Project No.: 115-2813-C-003-056-E
<br>Host Institution: Department of Electrical Engineering, National Taiwan Normal University
<br>Duration: 2026/07/01 ~ 2027/02/28

This scholarship, awarded by the National Science and Technology Council (NSTC), recognizes outstanding undergraduate students for their research proposals and supports them in conducting research projects.
